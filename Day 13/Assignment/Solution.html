Welcome to Phoebe's Clinic Management System

In this project. We have two user types : Patient and Doctor.

The following list are the functions patient and doctor can select from while accessing the clinic management system.

Patient

  • Book Appointment
  • View Appointment
  • Pay for Appointment
  • Cancel Appointment
  • Log out

Doctor

  • View Upcoming Appointment
  • View Past Appointment
  • Edit Details of Appointment
  • Log outt
